    FAQ Questions

    • What is Anokha TechFest?

      “Anokha Techfest, the annual event of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore provides an unique opportunity for students to participate in fun-filled and challenging events and workshops.

    • Why is Green Anokha concept?

      It was so wonderful to see the students adopt and implement our Chancellor AMMA’s idea of “Living in Harmony with Nature”. It was plastic-free and eco-friendly Anokha – an initiative that will become a tradition!!”, Dr. Sasangan Ramanathan, Dean, Faculty of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

    • How can I participate in Anokha 2022?

      You can register by visiting ANOKHA 2022 PAGE.

    • Do I need to register in advance?

      Registration is required to participate in the event. We recommend favoriting and/or adding a calendar invite for the keynote and other sessions to make sure you don’t miss out on the live experience.It requires advance registration, and space is limited.

    • What is the cost to attend Anokha 2022?

      All keynote and breakout sessions are free this year for Amrita students.For outside amrita people can pay Rs.500 and register.

    • How do I log on?

      Amrita students can login with their cms id and password after registration.

    • What if I miss a session or can’t attend a session

      Every session will be recorded and made available for on demand viewing soon after it ends! Catch up by clicking on past session titles in the schedule once they’re uploaded.
