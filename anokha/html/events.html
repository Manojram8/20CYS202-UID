    • Events and Shows

    “Anokha Techfest, the annual event of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore provides an unique opportunity for students to participate in fun-filled and challenging events and workshops. The event throws open opportunities for students from all disciplines to challenge themselves and foster the “Can Do” attitude. One of the key highlights of the Anokha 2020 edition was the Green Anokha concept. It was so wonderful to see the students adopt and implement our Chancellor AMMA’s idea of “Living in Harmony with Nature”. It was plastic-free and eco-friendly Anokha – an initiative that will become a tradition!!”, Dr. Sasangan Ramanathan, Dean, Faculty of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

    • 22
      FEBRUARY

      What is the event happening?

      TechExpo was another major attraction at Anokha 2019 which showcased a lot of state-of-the art technology including aircraft simulator, drone show and wild life photography. Exhibitors such as Roots, Mobil1 and Amrita departments & research centers such as Cyber Security, Cisco ThinQbator, etc. took part in TechExpo. School zone, an extensive school outreach program was also turned out to be a crowd puller. Designed to provide the children with a glimpse of the exciting technology of tomorrow, the event saw the participation of over 500 school children from various schools. School zone has provided school children with the opportunity to participate in 15 events such as School Wiz quiz, Marvel versus D, Sudoku, Sheldon Pictionary & Logic Magic coding contest.

      FEBRUARY

      What is the event happening?

      The mega event culminated with a scintillating Eventide Musical Show by Film fare award-winning playback singer, Mr. Naresh Iyer supported by Mr. Aalap Raju and Ms. Neha Venugopal. In addition, there were also spectacular cultural performances by Ragasudha and Natyasudha student clubs. The cultural activities included a campus short film contest named E-pictures and it was judged by Mr. Nag Ashwin, director of the acclaimed biopic, Mahanati. The organizing committee for Anokha 2019 of faculty members Mr. M. Sreevalsan, Prof. Prashant R. Nair & Dr. K. V. Shriram as Conveners and Mr. Gopakumar P., Mr. Baskar A., Mr. Arunkumar C., Mr. Ritwik M. & Mr. Vijay Narayanan as Co-Conveners.
